about me

Hi, I'm Vedashree Lavekar

A Computer Engineering student by degree, but a curious soul by nature. I'm the kind of person who stops mid-trail to admire clouds, chases soft breezes, and finds joy in the smallest things - a butterfly, a good meal, the first few minutes of rain.

I paint, I craft, I dance a little when the mood hits. I watch Asian dramas, obsess over superbikes I can't name yet, and live by one rule - we got one life, so enjoy every bit of it.

Coding and I didn't start as friends. I once called it "insects and bugs" - too small, too crowded, too confusing. But somewhere between my first project and my first internship, something shifted. Turns out coding is a lot like maths - learning the formula feels hard, but once you practice enough problems, it just clicks. And now I feel the fire.

I don't have a fixed picture of my future. I just know that it'll be full of surprise, growth, and things I haven't discovered yet - technical, creative, or completely unexpected.

Currently exploring. Always growing. Occasionally eating junk food.
